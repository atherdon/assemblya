## Drop system types

Target payload configurations:

1. 1 kg present
2. 2 kg present
3. 2.5 kg present
4. 2 small presents (simple dual drop)
5. Napalm / warm-gel dropper
6. Mines dropper
7. Vampire-like system (smaller form factor)
8. Infantry packages (multiple sizes)

Related notes also live under [Vampire-style system](./systems/vampire-style/readme.md) and competitor teardowns.

## Platform building blocks

- 3D-printed parts — see [3D models](./3d/readme.md)
- Drop system under the drone belly
- Sensors, wires, and activators
- One servo per present where practical

## Cost and scale goals

- Steal good ideas, lower unit cost
- Scale production toward 10× / 30× / 50×
- Own dual-present system
- Butterfly and IQOS-style dropper cages
- Different weights and sizes of presents
