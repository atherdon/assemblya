# Mission

## Why we are building this

We are not building AssemBLYA to maximize profit.

We are building it because better equipment saves lives, improves battlefield effectiveness, and helps Ukraine defend its land.

AssemBLYA is a veterans-led engineering project created by professionals from different fields — software, electronics, mechanical engineering, manufacturing, and battlefield operations.

Our goal is simple: build the best modular payload delivery platform while continuously reducing its cost.

Every new version should be:

- simpler
- more reliable
- easier to manufacture
- easier to repair
- significantly more affordable

## Philosophy

Good engineering should not be expensive.

Instead of maximizing margin, we invest in:

- scaling production
- testing
- research and development
- new payload systems
- manufacturing improvements

Additional profit is reinvested into making the next generation even better.

## Production model

We supply organizations, military units, and partners — not individual retail buyers.

AssemBLYA is available only through bulk procurement. That lets us:

- lower manufacturing costs
- standardize production
- improve logistics
- support continuous product development

## Long-term vision

The current release is only the first step.

We want a modular ecosystem that replaces many specialized payload delivery systems with one scalable platform:

- one platform
- multiple missions
- continuous improvement

The benchmark we aim for: reliability, modularity, and affordability in this category.
